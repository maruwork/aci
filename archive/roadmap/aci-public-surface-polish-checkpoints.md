# ACI Public Surface Polish Checkpoints

Status: Complete

## PSP-CP1 Public Surface Inventory

Pass when:
- public-facing files and routes are enumerated
- internal-temperature phrases are identified

## PSP-CP2 Public Entry Separation

Pass when:
- `README.md` presents the user route first
- internal roadmap / task / design reading routes are secondary and explicitly for maintainers

## PSP-CP3 Sample And Smoke Neutralization

Pass when:
- sample report uses neutral, reusable findings
- smoke output explanation matches actual output
- standalone and monorepo wording do not conflict

## PSP-CP4 Final Public Review

Pass when:
- a fresh read of README, quickstart, samples, and smoke output does not require internal history context
- remaining internal-facing surfaces are intentionally labeled
