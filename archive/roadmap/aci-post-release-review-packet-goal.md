# ACI Post-Release Review Packet Goal

Status: Complete

## Goal

Define one bounded post-release review packet that lets a maintainer review what happened after an `ACI` release without relying on oral explanation.

## Complete When

- one post-release review packet exists
- release-prep reading order points to that packet
- the packet defines minimum review fields and fail-close rules
- the packet is positioned after release execution and rollback discipline

## Out of Scope

- live incident response
- downstream repository fixes
- marketing or announcement copy

## Failure Conditions

- post-release learning still depends on scattered notes or chat memory
- the packet disagrees with release-note or rollback discipline
- the packet does not make outcome and follow-up work explicit
