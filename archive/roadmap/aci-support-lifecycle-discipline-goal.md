# ACI Support Lifecycle Discipline Goal

Status: Complete

## Goal

Define one explicit support lifecycle so `ACI` users and maintainers can tell what is supported, what is historical, and what belongs outside the generic tool boundary.

## Complete When

- one support lifecycle contract exists
- `SUPPORT.md` points to that contract
- support windows and required intake fields are explicit
- `README.md` and `docs/release/RELEASE_PREP_INDEX.md` route readers to the support lifecycle
- maintainer history and mirror sync include the wave

## Out of Scope

- live issue handling
- hosted support channels
- downstream repository-specific repair work

## Failure Conditions

- support promises remain implicit
- `SUPPORT.md` and versioning rules disagree
- users cannot tell whether a case belongs to generic `ACI` support
