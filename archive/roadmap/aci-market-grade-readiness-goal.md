# ACI Market-Grade Readiness Goal

Status: Complete

## Goal

Make `ACI` ready for the next product phase where it is judged against market tools, not only against internal common-shelf quality.

## Complete When

- missing product surfaces are explicitly classified into install/setup, runtime operation, triage/state, and trust/reliability
- the gap between current `ACI` and market-grade expectations is written as executable work, not as vague aspiration
- each missing surface has an owner boundary and a next implementation wave
- no major product gap remains hidden behind README quality or shelf cleanliness

## Out of Scope

- executing public release
- choosing a commercial packaging model
- building a hosted service

## Failure Conditions

- product gaps are mixed with already-closed shelf cleanup work
- tasks describe outcomes vaguely enough that another maintainer could not continue
- market-grade concerns are reduced to documentation polish only
